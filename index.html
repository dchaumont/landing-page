Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man!
After being bitten by a radioactive spider, teenager Peter Parker gains superhuman powers. Combined with his genius intellect and web-shooters, he protects the streets of New York City as the superhero Spider-Man!
Powers & Tech
Spider Sense alerts Spider-Man to any dangers around him.
Super Strength allows Spider-Man to lift over 25 tons.
Enhanced Agility allows Spider-Man to move and react with superhuman speed and precision.
Web Cartridges on his wrists allow Spider-Man to swing from buildings and spin all kinds of webs.
